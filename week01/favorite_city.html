Kwekwe, Zimbabwe

Kwekwe is located in the Midlands region of Zimbabwe, making it strategically positioned almost at the center of the country. This city is well known for its vibrant iron industry, which plays a crucial role in the local economy.

The city is not just an industrial hub but also boasts a rich cultural history and a lively community. With numerous shops and markets, Kwekwe offers a glimpse into the daily life of Zimbabweans.
